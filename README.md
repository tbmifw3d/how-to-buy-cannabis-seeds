## FAQ
### Is it legal to buy cannabis seeds online in 2026?
Legality depends on your location. In legalized areas like California or the Netherlands, yes—from licensed sellers. Elsewhere, it may be restricted to non-germination use.

### What should I look for in quality cannabis seeds?
Seek dark, hard shells, reputable breeders, and germination guarantees. Avoid cheap, unverified sources.

### How do I store cannabis seeds long-term?
Keep them in airtight containers in a fridge at 4-8°C; they can last 2-5 years.

### Can I buy cannabis seeds internationally?
Possible in some cases, but check import laws to avoid seizures or fines.
